Comprehensive training binder designed and organized to support flight instruction and pilot training. The binder contains structured lesson plans, diagrams, and explanations for key aviation topics. The fundamentals of instruction section is incomplete as I found the one provided by BackseatPilot was more comprehensive and better organized than the one I was creating, so I have been using that as a reference instead of creating my own. The rest of the binder is still in use and regularly updated with new material as I continue to develop my instructional skills and knowledge.
